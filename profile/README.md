## About MediaBubble

**MediaBubble** is a full-service digital marketing agency based in Hurghada, Egypt, established in 2015. We help businesses in Egypt and the UAE achieve sustainable growth through strategic, creative, and AI-powered marketing solutions.

We combine deep local market expertise with bilingual capabilities (English + Egyptian & Gulf Arabic) to deliver measurable results and strong ROI.

### Our Core Services

- **Search Engine Optimization (SEO)** – Technical, Local & Content Strategy  
- **Pay-Per-Click Advertising (PPC)** – Google Ads & Meta Ads with Smart Bidding  
- **Social Media Marketing** – Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn & Facebook  
- **Branding & Visual Identity** – Complete brand development and design  
- **Website Development** – High-converting websites and landing pages  
- **Content Marketing** – Arabic-first content creation and strategy  
- **AI Marketing Automation** – Advanced campaign orchestration and optimization
